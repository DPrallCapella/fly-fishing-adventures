Best Fishing Spots in Colorado

Colorado is home to some of the best fishing locations in the United States, offering a diverse range of environments, from mountain streams to vast reservoirs. Whether you’re an experienced angler or just starting, these top fishing spots provide opportunities to catch a variety of species including trout, bass, and more.

  • South Platte River - Known for its abundant trout population, this river offers both challenging and relaxing fishing experiences. The section near Deckers is particularly popular among anglers.
  • Cherry Creek Reservoir - A great spot for both boat and shore fishing, offering the chance to catch bass, catfish, and trout.
  • Blue River - Located near Silverthorne, the Blue River is famous for its trophy-sized trout and scenic surroundings, making it a top destination for fly fishing enthusiasts.
  • Eleven Mile Reservoir - A prime location for anglers looking for a variety of species including rainbow trout, brown trout, and pike. It’s also a great place for camping and outdoor activities.
  • Dream Stream - Situated between Spinney Mountain Reservoir and Eleven Mile Reservoir, Dream Stream is a legendary spot for catching large rainbow and brown trout, especially during spawning season.
  • Glenwood Springs - Famous for its hot springs, Glenwood Springs also offers fantastic fishing opportunities in the Colorado River and surrounding waters, with species like trout and smallmouth bass.
